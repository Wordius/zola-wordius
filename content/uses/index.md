- **Adobe**: InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator (Affinity is being tested as I write as a replacment for all three Adobe products; I also find Gimp useful occasionally) 
- **Mail client**: Mailspring (testing), [Airmail](https://airmailapp.com/) (iOS), Thunderbird (macOS)
- **Mail server**: iCloud+
- **Static site generator**: [Zola](https://getzola.org)
- **Writing**: Obsidian
- **Notes**: Logseq
- **To do**: Logseq, iOS Reminders
- **iPhone photo shooting**: Camera app
- **Photo management**: Apple Photos
- **Calendar:** Apple Calendar. It’s worth noting here that Thunderbird has a great calendar, whereby you can show a snapshot of the week ahead. You can display it in the right sidebar so that it doesn’t get in the way of your email
- **Cloud file storage:** iCloud
- **Contacts**: Apple Contacts
- **Browser:** Safari and Zen Browser
- **Chat**: Apple Messages, Signal
- **Bookmarks**: Raindrop.io
- **RSS**: NetNewsWire (~~Reeder~~ (iOS) and ~~ReadKit~~), ~~Omnivore~~
- **Word processing**: Obsidian
- **Spreadsheets**: Numbers
- **Shopping lists**: Pen and paper
- **Meal planning**: Paprika (recipes, occasional use)
- **Budgeting and personal finance**: Numbers
- **News**: When I have time, I look
- **Music**: Apple Music
- **Podcasts**: What are they?
- **Password management**: Bitwarden (~~1Password~~) and Apple Keychain
- **Mastodon**: Metatext and the browser
